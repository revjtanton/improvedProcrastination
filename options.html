Improved Procrastination

This extention will let you set a list of URL's you normally visit and waste time on. Then it will let you set a list of other URL's you also like to visit, but maybe you don't think of them first. Whenever you visit a site on the first list it will redirect you to a random URL on the second list. Sometimes it'll redirect you to the site you were trying to initially visit.

For Example:

If you list Facebook as a site you visit too often but codeFights is a site you should visit more often then when you visit Facebook you'll sometimes, randomly, go to codeFights instead.
